About BRICS Coin (BRX)

BRICS Coin (BRX) is a decentralized cryptocurrency inspired by the collaboration of the BRICS nations: Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa. Established to promote financial cooperation, the BRICS alliance holds annual summits discussing global development, trade, and multipolar governance. BRX aims to offer a unified digital asset for cross-border use among these emerging markets.
